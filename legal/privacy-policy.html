Privacy Policy for ParentTutor

Effective date: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ParentTutor ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the ParentTutor mobile app (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may process:

  • Technical data: basic device and app logs needed for troubleshooting.
  • Local app data: scan history, generated explanations, onboarding status, free scan count, and subscription status.
  • Homework images and related content: photos you capture or import, and extracted homework text used to generate tutoring help.
  • Profile information you enter: parent name and child name.

2. How We Use Information

  • Maintain security, debug issues, and improve reliability.
  • Operate subscriptions and app access features.
  • Save your recent results and preferences inside the app.
  • Provide core features such as homework analysis and tutoring guidance.

3. AI Processing and Third Parties

To analyze homework, the App sends image content you select to OpenAI for processing. This is required to generate answers and explanations. Your use of that processing is also subject to the provider's terms and privacy practices.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Content sent for AI analysis is transmitted over the network to our service provider.
  • Profile information, history, and app status are stored on your device using local storage.
  • You can remove local app data by clearing app data from your device or deleting history in app features where available.

5. Camera and Photo Access

We request camera and photo library permissions only so you can capture or import homework images for analysis.

6. Children's Privacy

ParentTutor is designed for use by parents and guardians to support learning. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without a parent or guardian's involvement.

7. Your Choices

  • You can revoke camera and photo permissions in your device settings.
  • You can remove locally stored app data from your device settings.
  • Do not upload images if you do not want them processed by OpenAI.

8. International Transfers

If you use the App from outside the country where our providers operate, your data may be transferred to and processed in other countries.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have privacy questions, contact us at openloopstudios@gmail.com.
