Help / FAQ

What is ParentTutor?
ParentTutor helps parents understand homework questions and get step-by-step explanations.

How do I use the app?
Open the app, enter or scan a homework question, and follow the guided explanation.

Which subjects are supported?
ParentTutor supports common school subjects such as math, science, and language-based homework help.

What if an answer looks incorrect?
AI-generated responses can be imperfect. Please verify important answers with school materials or a teacher.

How do I report an issue?
Email us with your device type, OS version, and a short description of the issue.

How do I request data deletion or privacy help?
Email us at the support address above with the subject line "Privacy Request".
